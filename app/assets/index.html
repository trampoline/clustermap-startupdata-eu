EU Startup Data

The EU Startup Data project will gather registry and local data from cities around the EU into a queryable resource covering startup activity across Europe.

Launch is planned in Q1 2015, initially including data from 8 countries. Throughout 2015 the dataset will be augmented with data from additional cities and countries.

The project is a collaboration between Grant Thornton and Trampoline Systems.
